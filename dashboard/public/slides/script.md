# slide 1
We’re Team ModelMasterz, and today we’re sharing how we tackled a real operational challenge: helping hotline operators act confidently on alerts sent by seniors through the Personal Alert Button system.
Our project, Personal Alert Plus, builds on existing workflows and adds an AI-assisted layer to help operators assess situations faster and respond with greater confidence

# slide 2
The existing challenge is two-fold.
First, on the senior side: the button is tied to a physical location. If something happens when they’re far from it, reaching the button itself becomes a challenge.
Even when an alert is triggered, audio can be difficult to interpret — because of language barriers, dialect differences, background noise, or incomplete recordings.

This then flows to the operator side, where they have to make decisions on many alerts at once, often with limited context.

# slide 3
We added two new modules on top of the existing PAB workflow.
First, an alternative mobile platform where seniors can trigger alerts, so help is still reachable even when they are not near the physical button.
Second, we added an AI-assisted assessment layer that provides early context and helps operators decide the appropriate response quickly.

# slide 4
This is how our AI supports the workflow in real time.
First, it understands incoming messages across different languages and dialects, and extracts distress keywords.
Second, using those signals together with context like medical history and past cases, it classifies urgency.
Third, when confidence is low, it triggers follow-up prompts to gather clearer signals.
Finally, it presents operators with consolidated context and confidence-backed insights, so they can review and make the final decision.

# slide 5
Before operators step in, the AI takes the first layer of action based on urgency.
This can include informing family members, sending follow-up prompts to seniors, and gathering additional details when a case appears urgent.
By the time the case reaches the operator, the system has already built a clearer picture to support faster and more accurate decisions.

# slide 6
For the live demo later, we’ll be focusing on two key flows: Urgent and Uncertain.
Urgent shows fast escalation when risk is high.
Uncertain shows how the system follows up, gathers more context, and reduces ambiguity before escalation.
Together, these two flows show how Personal Alert Plus improves both response speed and decision quality.

# slide 8
Our system is built on responsible AI for emergency support: AI recommends and priotize, humans stay in the loop, low-confidence safeguards are applied, and all actions are logged for full traceability.
