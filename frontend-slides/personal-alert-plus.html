A Smarter, AI-Driven Emergency Response System for Seniors.

The Challenge

Redefining the PAB (Personal Alert Button)

Traditional emergency response for seniors often faces bottlenecks in triage and language barriers, leading to delayed or incorrect responses.

  • High volume of false alarms overwhelming operators.
  • Critical information lost in translation (Dialects vs. English).
  • Delayed triage for life-threatening situations.

The Solution

Smart Intermediary Architecture

We decouple the response from fixed hardware by using a ubiquitous platform seniors already know—Telegram—and bridging it with advanced Gen AI triage.

📱 → 🤖 → 🖥️

Telegram Bot (Elderly) → Gen AI (Triage) → Operator Dashboard

The Entry Point

Multilingual Telegram Bot

Our bot recognizes the diverse languages used by seniors in our community:

  • Dialects: Hokkien, Cantonese.
  • National Languages: Malay, Tamil, Chinese.
  • Automatic Translation: All inputs are normalized to English for the database.
